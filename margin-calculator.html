Ecommerce Margin Calculator

What is profit margin?

Profit margin shows how much profit you make from each sale after subtracting the product cost. It helps ecommerce store owners understand if a product is profitable or not.

Formula

Margin % = (Selling price − Cost) ÷ Selling price × 100

Example

If a product sells for $50 and costs $20, the profit is $30 and the margin is 60%.
